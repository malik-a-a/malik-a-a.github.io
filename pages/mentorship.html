New Waves

New Waves was a project I started durring covid because I saw that there was starting to become a problem with kids health due to school PE being out of session and there were not many programs to get them active due to covid. This is when I decided to make my own mentorship/bootcamp program called New Waves which was outdoor and covid friendly. It was a huge sucsess and people kept on coming back for more. One specific student was 1 blood sugar point away from diebeties and above a healthy body weight. In 6 months he was not only far from diebetic, but went from not being close to being able to do a single pushup to being able to do 15-20 in one set!
